This page provides a convenient point of access for RFCs that are mentioned in the MicroMonitor documentation, plus others that are here for reference only.  Note that inclusion of an RFC in this list does not necessarily mean that the monitor follows the recommendations of that RFC.  Consider this page to be a super-set of RFCs that are related to the monitor.  As I refer to an RFC, if I think of it, I copy it to this site just for convenient future reference.
For a full listing of RFCs refer to ftp://ftp.ripe.net/rfc and a convenient search engine at  http://www.kashpureff.org/nic/search.html.
